# ADR 002: AI is a coach, not a grader

## Decision
AI Pair presents authored repair candidates. The optional Coach gives short investigation support without selecting an option or exposing the answer.

## Why
Learners need practice judging AI output. If AI grades itself, the main lesson disappears.

## Tradeoff
Guided help is intentionally limited. This may feel less convenient, but it protects the learning goal.
